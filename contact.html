Let's Connect

I'm always interested in meeting new people and discussing ideas around product development, game design, AI applications in creative work, or just about anything that intersects technology and creativity.

If you're working on something interesting, looking to collaborate, or just want to grab coffee in Brooklyn and chat, I'd love to hear from you. I'm particularly interested in connecting with:

  • Fellow game developers and indie creators
  • Product managers working on consumer-facing AI tools
  • Anyone exploring how AI can enhance creative processes
  • Musicians and creative collaborators in the NYC area
  • People building cool things who want to share their stories
