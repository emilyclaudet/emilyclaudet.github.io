About Me

I love building fun consumer experiences and video games. I'm interested in how AI can help people create their passion projects more easily. I try to keep up my own creative projects by launching small story based indie games and playing piano.

Currently Product lead for Consumer GenAI Lenses and Content at Snap, with a focus on AI model evaluations for Safety, Quality and Data Platform. Part time, working on an indie title Old and Gray as Creative Producer at Bolero Games Studio.

Prior to that I worked at Microsoft as a Senior PM for ML core typing quality for SwiftKey bringing smart mutlimodal features such as voice and predictive emoji and GIFs. Simultaneously I worked on my first indie title, Wisp Child part time in a team of 3 as a writer and game designer.
