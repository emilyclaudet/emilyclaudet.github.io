About Me

I'm interested in how AI can help people create things more easily, and in building consumer products that feel genuinely fun to use. I lead GenAI products at Snap, including AR Lenses, My AI chatbot, and AI safety and evaluation tooling.

Previously at Microsoft, where I led SwiftKey's typing intelligence for 100M+ users -- emoji prediction, voice typing, 100+ languages. Before that, London and Berlin startups: Kano (hardware kits teaching kids to code) and HelloFresh (delivery ops across 9 countries). I studied engineering at Cambridge.

On the side, I make indie games. I've shipped a narrative adventure on Steam, am producing a cozy pixel art game with Bolero Games Studio, and wrote a visual novel with my sister. I'll pick up whatever discipline a project needs -- product, design, code, narrative.

When I'm not doing any of that, I'm usually playing piano or being a good big sister and auntie.
