About Me

I love building fun consumer experiences and video games. I'm interested in how AI can help people create their passion projects more easily. I try to keep up my own creative projects by launching small story-based indie games and playing piano.

Current Work

Currently Product Lead for Consumer GenAI Lenses and Content at Snap, with a focus on AI model evaluations for Safety, Quality and Data Platform. Part time, working on an indie title Old and Gray as Creative Producer at Bolero Games Studio.

Previous Experience

Prior to that I worked at Microsoft as a Senior PM for ML core typing quality for SwiftKey bringing smart multimodal features such as voice and predictive emoji and GIFs. Simultaneously I worked on my first indie title, Wisp Child part time in a team of 3 as a writer and game designer.

Personal Interests

When I'm not working on product development or game design, I enjoy exploring the intersection of technology and creativity. I'm particularly fascinated by how AI tools can democratize creative processes and help more people bring their ideas to life. I also spend time playing piano, which helps me think through complex problems and provides a creative outlet outside of the digital world.

Living in Brooklyn has given me access to an amazing community of creators, developers, and entrepreneurs. I love meeting new people and discussing everything from the latest advances in AI to the nuances of narrative design in games.

If you're working on something interesting or just want to chat about technology, games, or music, I'd love to hear from you.
